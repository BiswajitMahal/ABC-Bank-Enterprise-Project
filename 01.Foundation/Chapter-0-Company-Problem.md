# Chapter 0 – Why Do Companies Move to Cloud?

## Objective

Understand the business problems that led organizations to adopt Cloud Computing.

## Business Scenario

ABC Bank wants to launch a new Internet Banking Platform.

Initially, the company plans to host everything inside its own data center.

## Challenges

* High infrastructure cost
* Hardware maintenance
* Limited scalability
* Power and cooling dependency
* Disaster Recovery complexity

## Solution

Cloud Computing enables organizations to consume infrastructure on demand instead of owning physical hardware.

## Key Learnings

* A server is simply a computer designed to provide services.
* A data center is a facility that hosts servers, networking devices, storage, and power infrastructure.
* Cloud solves business problems rather than simply providing virtual machines.
